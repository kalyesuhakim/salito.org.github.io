A Few Words About Us

We Voluntary Help for Almost 0 Years

Salito is a foundation in the Republic of Uganda which has come to the rescue of the future generations. It was stated by Ssali Brian and Zziwa Owen Steven the citzens of Uganda who grew up in hard times and wanted to make change in the way of life thus Salito

At Salito we provide scholarships to the needy children, provide scholarstic materials like pens etc. We fight against child abuse, for equality between the fortunate and less fortune.

We care for orphans and those with less fortune people and we teach them technical skills like capentry ,knitting and many other skills such that they can have a living to their expectations.
